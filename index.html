My Methodology

Just like a tea which has an immense taste has a recipe, here is my recipe which helps me to design and build the best experience as possible, provided the designed experiences are user-centric yes you read correctly user-centric. Now Let's Dive in.......

To Explore

I put myself in place of the user to understand their needs and empathize.

Ideate

We define together the framework of the experience and will begin to formalize the user journey.

Analyse

we will generate several ideas to solve user problems and then we do the competitive analysis.

Designate

we will try to give life to the solutions in the form of designs and interactive prototypes.


Test and Iterate

We will analyze the prototype with real users in order to optimize and validate the solution.


Deploy

Once the prototype has been validated. We can develop, launch and promote the product.
